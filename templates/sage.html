About SAGE

What is SAGE?

SAGE is a UW-Madison study abroad chatbot created to help students explore international programs that fit their interests. It uses information like your major, preferred country, and preferred language to match you with options that make sense academically and personally. The chatbot is designed to give students a simple and supportive way to learn about study abroad opportunities. Its goal is to make the decision process easier and less overwhelming.

What can SAGE do?

  • Recommend study abroad programs based on your major or academic goals.
  • Help you explore countries and languages you are interested in.
  • Explain program requirements, length, and general expectations.
  • Give guidance on what types of programs might be a good match for you.
  • Answer questions about the study abroad process and help you compare options.

How do I use it?

To use SAGE, type in your major, the countries or languages you're interested in, or any specific preferences you have. The chatbot will then suggest study abroad programs that align with your academic path and personal interests. You can ask follow-up questions or explore different options to see what fits you best. The more detail you provide, the more personalized your recommendations will be.
